Avocado Toast

Ingredients:

  • 1 avocado, mashed
  • 1 tsp. lemon juice
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 slices of bread, toasted
  • Hot sauce, to taste
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Lemon pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Mix lemon juice into mashed avocado
  2. Fry eggs to taste, I like over medium
  3. Spread avocado mixture on toast. Top with as much hot sauce as desired (I like Sriracha). Sprinkle on salt and pepper or lemon pepper.
  4. Put fried egg on top.
