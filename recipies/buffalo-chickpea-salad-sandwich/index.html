Buffalo Chickpea Salad Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 1 can chickpeas
  • 1/3 cup red bell pepper, minced
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup hot sauce (more/less to taste)
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt, to taste
  • Lettuce
  • Hamburger buns

Instructions

  2. Lightly mash chickpeas with a fork until hot sauce isnâ€™t so soupy
